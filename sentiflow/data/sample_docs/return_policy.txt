RETURN POLICY

Our company is committed to customer satisfaction. We offer a flexible return policy to ensure you're happy with your purchase.

Return Window:
- Standard items: 30 days from delivery date
- Electronics: 15 days from delivery date
- Custom or personalized items: Non-returnable

Return Conditions:
Items must be:
- In original condition and packaging
- Unused and unworn
- With all tags and labels attached
- Include original receipt or proof of purchase

How to Return:
1. Log into your account and go to Order History
2. Select the order and click "Return Items"
3. Choose the items you wish to return and select a reason
4. Print the prepaid return label
5. Pack items securely and attach the label
6. Drop off at any authorized shipping location

Refund Processing:
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return
- Original payment method will be credited
- Shipping costs are non-refundable (except for defective items)
- Return shipping is free for defective or incorrect items

Exchanges:
We don't offer direct exchanges. Please return the original item and place a new order for the replacement.

Defective Items:
If you receive a defective item:
- Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
- We'll send a replacement immediately
- Return shipping is fully covered

Contact Us:
For return questions, email returns@example.com or call 1-800-RETURNS
Our customer service team is available Monday-Friday, 9am-6pm EST.
